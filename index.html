Our Legacy

Established in 1987 as Horizon Builders & Developers, we began with construction of turn-key residential complexes, infrastructure projects, and commercial buildings. Over three decades, the organisation evolved — from Horizon Ventures Pvt. Ltd. to Ventura Realtors India Pvt. Ltd. — with a singular focus: building affordable, high-quality homes for the discerning home buyer in Kochi.

The company's legacy is hundreds of satisfied homeowners in and around Kochi who chose us to be part of their journey.
